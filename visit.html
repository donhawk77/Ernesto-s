Before You Go

Good to Know

Dress Code

Smart casual. We're a gourmet restaurant with a welcoming atmosphere — come dressed to enjoy a special meal, but no jacket required.

Dietary Accommodations

We're happy to accommodate dietary needs. Please let your server know about allergies or restrictions. Many dishes can be modified for gluten-free or vegetarian preferences.

Payment

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. We do not accept personal checks.

Large Groups

For parties of 8 or more, please call ahead to ensure we can accommodate your group. Private dining options are available for 10–50 guests. Learn more →

Takeout & Pickup

Full menu available for takeout. Call (210) 344-1248 to place your order. Typical preparation time is 20–30 minutes.

Special Occasions

Celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or promotion? Let us know when you reserve and we'll make it special. Complimentary dessert for birthday celebrations.
